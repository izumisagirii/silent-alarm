SilentAlarm is an alarm app that only plays through earphones, never through speakers, so you never wake others.

It is built with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose, and uses aggressive process-keeping techniques to survive OEM killers.

Features:
- Multi-alarm support with one-shot and recurring schedules
- Earphone-only routing for wired, Bluetooth, and USB headsets
- Bluetooth wake-up handling to prevent audio truncation
- Fallback modes for vibration-only or speaker playback when no earphones are connected
- Separate earphone and speaker volume controls per alarm
- Custom ringtone selection with persisted storage across reboots
- Quick Settings tile to toggle all alarms from the control center

SilentAlarm is a privacy-focused alarm clock designed for people who do not want their alarms to play out loud.
